Learning Hub

Centralized repository for all field technician resources, providing quick access to job aids, training videos, 3D equipment models, and interactive simulations. Content is organized by priority based on field usage data.

Access job aids, videos, 3D models, and simulations to support your work in the field

Search Functionality

Specialized search for Learning Hub content with filters for content type and language, optimized for field technicians needing to quickly find resources. Results are ranked by relevance and popularity.

Content Type Filter

Filter content by specific resource types to quickly find exactly what you need: job aids (documents), videos (demonstrations), 3D models (equipment visualization), or simulations (interactive training).

Language Filter

Filter content by language preference. Many resources are available in both English and French to support our bilingual workforce.

Quick Access
Quick Access Navigation

One-click navigation to most commonly used resource categories. This design reduces the number of clicks needed to access critical content, based on user research showing technicians need faster access to resources while on service calls.
